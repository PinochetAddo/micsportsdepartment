ABOUT US

Morgan International Community school started sports in 2014-2015. We had introduced inter house competition, we incorporated playing friendly matches with public school, like Swesbu, Swedru and many others and this alone gave us the opportunity to become members of International Schools Sports & Athletics Game (ISSAG) which was an achievement for us. Through our local practice and other factors, we went ahead to join the international school body where we had the opportunity to compete with other colleges of other international schools.
